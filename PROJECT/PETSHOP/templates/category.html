Our Story

petZworld began with humble beginnings and a single store. It grew slowly and organically through the years, now we have 6 stores across Mumbai and a pan India presence with our website. Our aim has always been to provide the largest range of pet products at reasonable prices and make the shopping experience comfortable for pet parents. We are a family run business that has built its online presence through the foundation of the brick and mortar stores.
